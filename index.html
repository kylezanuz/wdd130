Philippines

Luneta Shrine in the Philippines, also known as Rizal Park, is one of the largest urban parks in Asia, covering around 58 hectares. It’s famous for the Rizal Monument, which stands about 12.7 meters tall, and it marks the exact spot where Dr. José Rizal was executed on December 30, 1896.
